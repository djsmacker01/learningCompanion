Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

We use cookies to enhance your experience. This Cookie Policy explains how Learning Companion uses cookies and similar technologies.
1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve your user experience
  • Analyze how you use our platform
  • Provide personalized content and features
2. Types of Cookies We Use
2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They include:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery Session
user_preferences Stores your accessibility and display preferences 1 year
2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our platform:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - session state 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme_preference Remembers your theme choice (light/dark) 1 year
language Stores your language preference 1 year
study_reminders Remembers your study reminder settings 6 months
2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaigns:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_fbp Facebook Pixel - tracks conversions 3 months
_gcl_au Google Ads - conversion tracking 3 months
3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Google Ads: For advertising and conversion tracking
  • Facebook Pixel: For social media advertising
  • Intercom: For customer support and messaging
  • Stripe: For payment processing (if applicable)
4. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

We also use similar technologies to cookies:

  • Local Storage: Stores larger amounts of data on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage for your current session
  • IndexedDB: For offline data storage and synchronization
  • Web Beacons: Small images that track email opens and page views
5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies for the best experience
  • Essential Only: Only allow necessary cookies
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow
  • Manage Later: Change your preferences at any time
6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences
6.1 Through Our Website

You can manage your cookie preferences by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer
  • Going to your account settings (if logged in)
  • Using our cookie consent banner
6.2 Through Your Browser

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

  • Login Issues: You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Lost Preferences: Your settings may not be remembered
  • Limited Personalization: Content may not be customized
  • Analytics: We cannot improve our service based on usage data
8. Mobile App Cookies

Our mobile app may use similar technologies:

  • App Analytics: Track app usage and performance
  • Crash Reporting: Identify and fix app issues
  • Push Notifications: Send relevant updates and reminders
  • Offline Sync: Store data for offline access
9. Data Protection and Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy:

  • Minimal Data: We only collect necessary information
  • Secure Storage: Cookies are stored securely
  • No Personal Data: Cookies do not contain personal information
  • Regular Review: We regularly review our cookie usage
10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated date
  • Communicated through our platform
  • Notified via email for significant changes
